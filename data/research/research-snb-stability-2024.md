# Financial Stability Report

**Institution:** SNB (Schweizerische Nationalbank)  
**Autoren:** Swiss National Bank  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://www.snb.ch/en/publications/financial-stability-report

## Abstract
Assessment of Swiss financial sector stability covering banks, insurance, and fintech. Examines digitalization risks and opportunities, including AI adoption in financial services and cybersecurity implications.

## Key Findings

- Swiss banks investing heavily in AI for risk management and compliance
- Fintech sector growing, 400+ startups in Crypto Valley and beyond
- AI-driven fraud detection becoming standard practice
- Cybersecurity risks increasing with AI-powered attacks
- Regulatory framework adapting to AI in financial services
- Employment in traditional banking declining, fintech hiring increasing

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
Direkter Bezug zum Schweizer Finanzplatz. SNB beobachtet Transformation des Bankensektors durch AI. Zürich und Genf als Finanzzentren stark betroffen von Automatisierung.
