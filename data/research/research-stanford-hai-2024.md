# AI Index Report 2024

**Institution:** Stanford HAI (Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI)  
**Autoren:** Stanford Institute for Human-Centered AI  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://aiindex.stanford.edu/report/

## Abstract
Annual comprehensive report tracking AI progress across research, industry, policy and public perception. Covers model capabilities, investment trends, regulation developments, and labor market impacts globally.

## Key Findings 2024

### Technical Performance
- AI exceeds human performance on many benchmarks
- Frontier models becoming increasingly powerful
- Cost of training falling significantly
- Open-source models closing gap with proprietary

### Industry Adoption
- Private AI investment: $95.99 billion in 2023
- Generative AI investment: $25.2 billion
- US leads, followed by China and UK
- Switzerland ranks high in AI talent density

### Labor Market Impact
- AI adoption accelerating across industries
- Knowledge work most affected
- New roles emerging faster than old roles disappearing
- Skills gap widening globally

### Policy & Regulation
- Significant increase in AI regulations worldwide
- EU AI Act as landmark legislation
- Focus on safety and responsible AI
- International coordination increasing

## Relevanz für Schweiz
- Hohe KI-Forschungsqualität (ETH, EPFL)
- Starke Position in KI-Talent
- Finanzsektor früher Adopter
- Regulatorischer Rahmen in Entwicklung
