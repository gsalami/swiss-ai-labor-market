# Future of Work - Global Commission Report

**Institution:** ILO (International Labour Organization)  
**Autoren:** International Labour Organization  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://www.ilo.org/global/topics/future-of-work/lang--en/index.htm

## Abstract
UN agency analysis of global labor market transformation through technology, demographics, and climate change. Provides framework for human-centered approach to future of work including social protection and lifelong learning.

## Key Findings

- Technology not destiny - policy choices shape outcomes
- Lifelong learning investment critical for workforce adaptation
- Social protection systems need modernization for gig economy
- Human-AI collaboration preferable to replacement paradigm
- Green transition creates significant job opportunities
- Quality of work as important as quantity of jobs
- Universal basic skills guarantee proposed

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
ILO-Empfehlungen relevant für Schweizer Sozialpolitik. Starkes Berufsbildungssystem als Vorteil für Lifelong Learning. Flexicurity-Ansatz kompatibel mit Schweizer Arbeitsmarktmodell.
