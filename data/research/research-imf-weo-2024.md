# World Economic Outlook

**Institution:** IMF (International Monetary Fund)  
**Autoren:** International Monetary Fund  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://www.imf.org/en/Publications/WEO

## Abstract
Global economic analysis examining macroeconomic trends, growth forecasts, and emerging challenges. Includes special chapters on AI's impact on productivity and labor markets across advanced economies.

## Key Findings

- AI could boost productivity growth by 0.4-0.8 percentage points annually in advanced economies
- Estimated 40% of global employment exposed to AI, higher in advanced economies (60%)
- AI may worsen income inequality without proper policy interventions
- Complementarity between AI and human workers crucial for positive outcomes
- Advanced economies like Switzerland face both highest potential gains and transition challenges
