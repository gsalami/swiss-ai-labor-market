# The economic potential of generative AI

**Institution:** McKinsey Global Institute  
**Autoren:** McKinsey Global Institute  
**Jahr:** 2023  
**URL:** https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-economic-potential-of-generative-ai-the-next-productivity-frontier

## Abstract
Comprehensive analysis of how generative AI could transform productivity across industries. Estimates generative AI could add $2.6-4.4 trillion annually to the global economy. Examines impact on 850 occupations and 2,100 work activities.

## Key Findings

- Generative AI could add $2.6-4.4 trillion annually to the global economy
- 75% of value from generative AI comes from four areas: customer operations, marketing/sales, software engineering, R&D
- Knowledge workers will be most affected, not manual labor
- About 60-70% of workers' time could be automated with current technology
- Banking, high tech, and life sciences have highest automation potential

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
Die Schweiz als Hochlohnland mit starkem Dienstleistungssektor ist besonders betroffen. Finanzsektor (Zürich, Genf) und Pharma (Basel) stehen im Fokus der Transformation.
