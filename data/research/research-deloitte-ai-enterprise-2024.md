# State of AI in the Enterprise

**Institution:** Deloitte  
**Autoren:** Deloitte AI Institute  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://www2.deloitte.com/us/en/pages/consulting/articles/state-of-ai-in-the-enterprise.html

## Abstract
Enterprise AI adoption survey examining how businesses implement and scale AI solutions. Covers organizational readiness, implementation challenges, ROI metrics, and workforce transformation strategies.

## Key Findings

- 79% of enterprises expect AI to transform their industry within 3 years
- Only 25% of AI pilots successfully scale to production
- Top barriers: data quality, talent shortage, integration complexity
- Generative AI adoption accelerating - 42% of companies already deploying
- AI leaders see 30% higher revenue growth than laggards
- Workforce reskilling most critical success factor

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
Schweizer Grossunternehmen (UBS, Novartis, Nestlé) sind AI-Vorreiter. KMU-Landschaft zeigt jedoch Nachholbedarf. Fachkräftemangel besonders akut in der Schweiz.
