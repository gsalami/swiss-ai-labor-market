# Global AI Study: Sizing the Prize

**Institution:** PwC  
**Autoren:** PwC  
**Jahr:** 2024  
**URL:** https://www.pwc.com/gx/en/issues/artificial-intelligence.html

## Abstract
Economic impact analysis of AI adoption worldwide. Projects AI contribution to global GDP, examines sector-specific opportunities, and analyzes regional variations in AI readiness and economic benefits.

## Key Findings

- AI projected to contribute $15.7 trillion to global GDP by 2030
- Labor productivity gains account for over 55% of economic benefits
- Healthcare and automotive sectors see largest relative gains
- Western Europe expected to see 9.9% GDP boost from AI
- Consumer demand for personalized products driving 45% of gains
- Switzerland among top 10 countries for AI economic readiness

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
PwC stuft die Schweiz als eines der AI-readiest Länder ein. Starke Finanz- und Pharmaindustrie positioniert für überdurchschnittliche Gewinne. BIP-Boost von 10-12% bis 2030 möglich.
