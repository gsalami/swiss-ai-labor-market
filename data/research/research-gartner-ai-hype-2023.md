# Gartner Hype Cycle for Artificial Intelligence

**Institution:** Gartner  
**Autoren:** Gartner  
**Jahr:** 2023  
**URL:** https://www.gartner.com/en/articles/what-s-new-in-artificial-intelligence-from-the-2023-gartner-hype-cycle

## Abstract
Technology maturity assessment of AI innovations. Tracks generative AI, foundation models, and emerging AI technologies through innovation trigger, peak of inflated expectations, trough of disillusionment, and plateau of productivity.

## Key Findings

- Generative AI at Peak of Inflated Expectations, plateau in 2-5 years
- Foundation models reaching enterprise maturity
- AI engineering practices becoming essential
- Autonomous systems still early stage (5-10 years to mainstream)
- AI governance tools emerging as critical need
- Composite AI approaches gaining traction

## Relevanz für die Schweiz
Für Schweizer Unternehmen wichtig: Realistische Erwartungen an AI-Projekte setzen. Generative AI kurzfristig nutzbar, autonome Systeme erfordern längerfristige Planung.
